OceanOne for an Unforgettable Experience

Situated on Sri Lanka’s Southern Coastline, within a short distance from Galle, Unawatuna and Talpe, the peaceful beach hamlet of OceanOne is full of picture-perfect scenery and quiet coastal paths. Relatively un-crowded palm-lined beaches, quaint temples and the islet-dotted OceanOne Lake mesmerize those seeking to escape the humdrum of city life. Colourful coral reefs draw the snorkeling enthusiast. Instagrammable stilt fishermen dot the breathtaking beaches of nearby Ahangama.
