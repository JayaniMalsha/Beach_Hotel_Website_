About Us



Welcome to OceanOne Beach Hotel, a haven of tranquility and luxury located on the pristine beaches of Sri Lanka. Founded in 2001, our hotel has been a symbol of excellence in hospitality for over two decades.


Our History


OceanOne Beach Hotel was established with the vision of providing an unparalleled beachfront experience to travelers from around the world. Over the years, we have grown and evolved, continually enhancing our facilities and services to meet the needs of our discerning guests.


Our Mission


Our mission is to create memorable experiences for our guests by offering exceptional service, luxurious accommodations, and a deep connection with the natural beauty of our surroundings. We strive to be a sanctuary where guests can relax, rejuvenate, and create lasting memories.


Our Values


  • Excellence: We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of service and hospitality.

  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and respect.

  • Sustainability: We are dedicated to preserving the natural beauty of our environment and minimizing our ecological footprint.

  • Community: We support and engage with the local community, promoting cultural heritage and economic development.
